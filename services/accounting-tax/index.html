Key Deadlines

UAE Tax Compliance Calendar

Miss these dates and you’ll face FTA penalties. We track every deadline for every client and file before the deadline — not on it.

Ongoing — Quarterly
VAT Return Filing
Due on the 28th of the month following each tax period. Most UAE businesses file quarterly: Jan–Mar return due 28 Apr; Apr–Jun due 28 Jul; Jul–Sep due 28 Oct; Oct–Dec due 28 Jan.
VAT
Annual — 9 months after year end
Corporate Tax Return
CT returns must be filed within 9 months of your financial year end. For companies with a December year end, the CT return is due by 30 September of the following year.
Corporate Tax
Within 30 days of registration
FTA Tax Registration
Businesses exceeding AED 375,000 annual turnover must register for VAT within 30 days of crossing the threshold. CT registration must be done within 3 months of the financial year end.
Registration
License renewal period
Audited Financial Statements
Most free zones (DMCC, JAFZA, DIFC, and others) require audited financial statements as part of annual license renewal. Audit must be completed before renewal deadline.
Annual Audit

Payroll & WPS Filing
All UAE employers must process salaries through the Wage Protection System (WPS) by the last working day of each month. Non-compliance results in labour quota freezes.

VAT Deregistration
If your turnover falls below AED 187,500 or you close your business, you must deregister for VAT within 20 days of becoming eligible. Failure to deregister results in continued VAT obligations.
