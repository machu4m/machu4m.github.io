How It Works

Trademark Registration:
Step by Step

Our most-requested corporate service. Here’s exactly how we handle it — the same thorough process applies to all our corporate services.

1
Day 1 · Free
Trademark search & clearance

Before filing anything, we search the UAE Trademark Registry and international databases to confirm your mark is available. Conflicts are identified early — before you invest in filing fees. We provide a written clearance report.

2
Week 1
Class selection & application preparation

We identify the correct Nice Classification classes for your goods or services (45 classes exist) and prepare a precise application. Incorrect class selection is the most common and costly mistake in DIY trademark filings.

3
Week 1–2
Ministry of Economy submission

We file your application with the UAE Ministry of Economy Trademark Office. You receive a filing confirmation and official application number. Your priority date is established from this moment — protecting your claim.

4
Month 2–5
Examination & publication

The Trademark Office examines your application for distinctiveness and conflicts. If approved, it’s published in the Official Gazette. We monitor the 30-day opposition window and handle any objections filed against your mark.

5
Month 6–12
Registration certificate issued

Once the opposition period passes without challenge (or challenges are resolved), your trademark is registered. We collect your official registration certificate and deliver a complete IP portfolio document.

6
Year 10
Renewal & ongoing protection

UAE trademarks are valid for 10 years and renewable indefinitely. We set renewal reminders and handle the renewal process. We also advise on enforcement if infringement is detected — including UNODC customs filing for border protection.
