The eTrade License is issued by Dubai DET specifically for businesses operating entirely online — selling via website, Instagram, Amazon, Etsy, WhatsApp or any digital channel. At AED 2,070/year it’s the cheapest legal UAE license available. It does not include a UAE residency visa and cannot be used to open a physical shop, trade B2B in person, or employ staff. It’s ideal for solo digital entrepreneurs, social media sellers and online consultants.
Our AED 9,800 all-inclusive package covers: the free zone trade license (1–3 activities), one investor visa, Emirates ID application, share certificate and company documentation. Available through RAKEZ (RAK), SHAMS (Sharjah) or IFZA (Dubai) depending on your activity. Medical examination fee (AED 320–500) is separate. Free zone companies cannot directly sell to UAE mainland clients — they’re designed for international trade. Workarounds: (1) Use a local UAE distributor who sells on your behalf; (2) Set up a dual structure — free zone for international, mainland for UAE local; (3) If selling online to UAE consumers, a free zone company can often work with some limitations. We advise the right structure in the free consultation.
For free zone and offshore companies: No. License, company documents and bank account introduction can all be handled 100% remotely. You only need to visit the UAE if you want a residency visa (for medical and Emirates ID biometrics — typically a 3–5 day visit). For mainland companies: most documents are prepared remotely but final signing often requires a brief UAE visit or a power of attorney. eTrade license: fully remote.
Processing times: Dubai free zones (IFZA, Dubai South) issue licenses in 24–72 hours. SHAMS and SAIF Zone take 24–48 hours. RAKEZ takes 1–3 days. Abu Dhabi free zones typically 3–7 days. Any mainland license takes 3–7 business days once documents are complete. eTrade license can be issued within 24 hours. UAE residency visa (if included) adds 2–3 weeks for medical, biometrics and Emirates ID. RAK ICC offshore takes 2–4 days.
